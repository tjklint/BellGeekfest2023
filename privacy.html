Privacy Policy - Privacy Xpresso

Effective Date: October 14, 2023

At Privacy Xpresso, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your online presence remains under your control. Our mission is to help individuals maintain their digital privacy and clear their name from the internet. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information and maintain transparency about our practices.

1. Information We Don't Collect:

Privacy Xpresso is committed to not collecting any personal data or using cookies. We do not gather, store, or process any information related to your identity, online activities, or personal preferences. Your privacy is our utmost priority.

2. Use of Our Services:

While you use Privacy Xpresso's services, we do not track your actions, gather data, or store any information on our servers or back-end systems.

3. Email Communication:

If you contact us through email, we may collect your email address solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries or providing assistance. We do not use your email address for any other purpose or share it with third parties.

4. Security:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any limited information that may be received during communication. However, as our primary commitment is to not collect data, there is minimal information that needs safeguarding.

5. Third-Party Services:

Privacy Xpresso's services are designed to minimize third-party involvement and data exchange. We do not share information with third-party entities, and our services do not rely on external data sources.

6. Policy Updates:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. When we do so, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the beginning of the policy.

7. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, our practices, or your interactions with Privacy Xpresso, please contact us at timothyjklint@gmail.com.

Privacy Xpresso is dedicated to providing individuals with the means to regain control of their online presence while respecting their right to privacy. We remain committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and security.

Thank you for entrusting us with your privacy needs.
